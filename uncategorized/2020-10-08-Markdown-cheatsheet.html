Heading 4

Emphasized text

Markup :  _Emphasized text_ or *Emphasized text*

Strikethrough text

Strong text

Markup :  __Strong text__ or **Strong text**

Strong emphasized text

Markup :  ___Strong emphasized text___ or ***Strong emphasized text***
